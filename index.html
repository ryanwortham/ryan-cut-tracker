Daily Targets

Cutting Calories

  • Training days: 2,550-2,750 calories
  • Rest days: 2,300-2,450 calories
  • Protein: 250-260g/day
  • Adjust only after two full weeks of average-weight trend data.

Training Day Macros

Lift + Cardio Days

  • Protein: 250-260g
  • Carbs: 230-280g
  • Fat: 55-70g
  • Put more carbs around training and keep dinner lean.

Rest Day Macros

Recovery Days

  • Protein: 250-260g
  • Carbs: 120-170g
  • Fat: 70-85g
  • Keep steps high and use vegetables to stay full.

7-Day Meal Plan

High protein, controlled carbs

Pizza World Approved

Work Meals

  • Grilled chicken salad, dressing measured on the side
  • Chicken breast or lean protein with vegetables
  • Turkey, chicken, or lean beef bowl without extra cheese
  • Protein shake when the shift gets chaotic
  • Zero-calorie drink or water only

Pizza Rules

Controlled Exposure

  • No grazing from the line, pans, boxes, or slices.
  • If pizza is planned, log it in 1st Phorm before eating.
  • Keep it to a measured portion and pair it with lean protein.
  • No emotional slices after stressful shifts.

Avoid

Cut Killers

  • Soda, sweet tea, energy drinks with sugar, juice
  • Untracked pizza, breadsticks, wings, desserts
  • Liquid calories and random sauces
  • Late-night drive-thru meals

Grocery List

Keep it boring and repeatable
  • Chicken breast
  • Lean ground beef or turkey
  • Egg whites and whole eggs
  • Greek yogurt
  • Protein powder
  • Rice, potatoes, oats
  • Low-carb wraps
  • Vegetables and salad kits
  • Berries and bananas
  • Olive oil spray, salsa, zero-cal sauces
